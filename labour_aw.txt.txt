ANNUAL LEAVE: According to Section 18 of the Nigerian Labour Act, every employee who has worked for 12 months is entitled to a holiday with full pay of at least 6 working days.

TERMINATION: Section 11 states that notice must be given before termination, ranging from one day to one month depending on the length of service.

MATERNITY LEAVE: Female employees are entitled to 12 weeks of maternity leave with at least 50% of their salary if they've worked for the employer for at least 6 months.
